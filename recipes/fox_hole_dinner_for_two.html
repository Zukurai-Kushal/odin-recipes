Fox Hole Dinner for Two

Description: No one likes to dine alone, and this recipe is ideal to combine a variety of C-Ration Units. *This is from your Basic C-Ration
    Ingredients:
  • Two spoons butter or oil or fat
  • Two spoons flour
  • *One can chicken and noodles
  • *One can turkey load, cut up into pieces
  • Three dashes TABASCO pepper sauce
  • *Salt and pepper to taste
  • *One can cheese spread
  • *12 spoons milk
  • *Crackers from one C-Ration can, or crumbled
    Instructions:
  1. Melt butter or oil or fat, add flour and stir until smooth. Add milk and continue to cook until sauce begins to thicken. Add cheese spread and cook until cheese melts and sauce is even.
  2. Empty cans of turkey loaf and chicken noodles into the cheese sauce. Season with Tabasco, salt and pepper to taste and continue cooking.
  3. Cover poulette with crumbled crackers and serve piping hot.
